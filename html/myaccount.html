Help (FAQs)

How do I place an order online?

To place an order, simply navigate to our website, select the "Order Now" button, choose your desired items from the menu, and proceed to checkout. You can also place orders through our mobile app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and sometimes cash on delivery. You can find specific details at the checkout.

Can I customize my burger order?

Absolutely! You can customize your burger by adding or removing ingredients. Just select the "Customize" option while placing your order online.

What are your delivery options and fees?

We offer delivery within a specified radius from our locations. Delivery fees may vary based on distance. For more information, check our delivery details on the website.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we do! We have a variety of vegetarian and vegan burger options on our menu. Look for specific labels or inquire while placing your order.

What if I have food allergies?

We take allergies seriously. While we strive to accommodate allergies, please inform us while placing your order and consult our allergen information available on the website.

Is there a minimum order amount for delivery?

Minimum order requirements for delivery may vary based on location. Check the website or app for specific information regarding minimum order amounts..

What is your refund or return policy?

We aim for customer satisfaction. In the event of an issue with your order, please contact our customer support team, and we'll work to resolve it promptly.

Are nutritional details available for your menu items?

Yes, we provide nutritional information for our menu items. You can find this information on our website or by requesting it from our customer support.
